Draughtsman Civil Engineering

Draughtsman (Civil) is a working art vocational trade. The duration of trade is two years with four semesters of six months each. Students are introduced to sketches, references and specifications skills to translate ideas; and into detailed technical drawings showing plans, elevations and sections in blueprints which the construction crew use to create the complex and realistic sets you see on the cinema screen. The trade course is very important and career orienting in nature and they can find so many jobs in various art areas.
Duration:2 years
Stream:Engineering


Eligibility criteria

  • 10th class pass is minimum qualification for this trade.

Benefits

  • After passing trade, students have so many art jobs in various government protected buildings and the like other historical places.
  • Students can go for further higher degree courses in arts fields.
  • They can also have jobs related to drawing patterns about things of art like historical or fine arts.

Advance Courses in Draughtsman Civil Engineering Trade

  • Diploma in Architectural Draughtsman
  • Post Graduate in Diploma in Architectural Draughtsmanship
