Secretarial Practice


Secretarial Practice (English) is an executive and administrative vocational trade. The duration of trade is one year with two semesters of six months each. The trade course comprises mainly shorthand, typewriting, Secretarial services, general English, business correspondence, general knowledge, current affairs and other administrative processes. There are many ITIs in the country offering this trade due to its good job scope in administration both government and private sectors.
Duration:1 year
Stream:Non-Engineering


Eligibility criteria

  • Students should be passed in 10th class examination from a recognised school board in the country.

Benefits

  • Students after passing trade successfully, they can work as private secretary or personal assistant in multi-national companies, private offices, banks, institutes, colleges and schools, social organization etc.
  • Students can work in stock exchanges, the Department of Company Affairs, company law boards and various government departments.
  • Students with good academic records can go for further studies like Diploma/Bachelor Degree courses as this enhances their overall qualification.
