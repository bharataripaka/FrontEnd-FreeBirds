Humanities And Others Vocational Courses After 10th Class


Why should I choose humanities

Work-related learning. The humanities are vocational. ... Humanities and seemingly more vocational disciplines like engineering or computing each offer mastery of particular areas of life in our complex world. All teach students to engage with the real world, imparting knowledge and skills of practical use

Humanities help you understand your responsibilities towards culture and society. You get knowledge about different languages, cultures, and histories of various traditions. This helps to understand how different policies are made by the government and how one can improve society by making policies at different levels


Eligibility Criteria

  • The student must have completed class 10th from a school that comes under a recognised board of education.
  • The aggregate score of the candidate in class 10th board exams . Some relaxation in aggregate marks is provided at most of the colleges to the students belonging to reserved categories
  • The candidate must have received passing marks in all the subjects of class 10th.
  • The time duration is two years

Must Have Skills for Career in Humanities/Arts after Class 10

  • A student planning to take up Humanities/Arts should have a creative bent of mind
  • They should be receptive to new thoughts, ideas and perspectives
  • They should have a seeking spirit
  • A source of good, healthy imagination is always a plus
  • They must be willing to develop their communication skills
  • They should be dedicated to their subjects, and have a knack for theoretical learning
