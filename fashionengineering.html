Diploma in Fashion Engineering


Students can pursue an Advanced Diploma in Fashion Designing, Diploma in Fashion Designing or Post Graduate Diploma in Fashion Design. These courses touch topics such as garment construction, merchandising, advertising, the progress and proposal of fashion ideas. The students also gain the ability to build a fashion portfolio. A Diploma in fashion designing also aims at teaching the practical skills that can help an individual in his career in fashion designing.

Students willing to enter the world of fashion designing get to learn about the vast universe of fashion. They get to learn about creativity and innovation in fashion designing. Also, the diploma courses in fashion designing introduce aspirants to the technical aspects. Students are supported to work on skills like drafting, illustration, fashion styling, sewing, and garment & design construction..


Eligibility criteria

  • The student must have completed class 10th from a school that comes under a recognised board of education.
  • The aggregate score of the candidate in class 10th board exams must be at least 50%. Some relaxation in aggregate marks is provided at most of the colleges to the students belonging to reserved categories
  • The candidate must have received passing marks in all the subjects of class 10th.
  • The time duration is three years

Board

To help speed up the process of development of Technical Education, a Board in the name and style of "STATE BOARD OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING" was created vide G.O.Ms.No.371 Edn-dated 26.2.1957. The Joint Director (Technical Education) of DPI used to act as secretary of this newly formed Board. The Board is formed as a statutory body under the A.P. Education Act-1982 (act 1 of 1982), an act of assembly, in the present form, vide G.O.Ms.No.140 dated 24.4.1984.
