Information Technology & E.S.M. Engineering


1 Information Technology & Electronics System Maintenance is an information and electronics system maintaining vocational trade. The duration of trade is two years with four semesters of six months each. Students are introduced to so many topics during teaching such as install, troubleshoot, repair and maintain electronic systems and equipment used in industrial manufacturing; checking for loose connections and defective components on electronic control systems and mechanical equipment; fixing a problem and evaluate computer diagnostic tests. Trade is an important from job point of view as it opens wide career areas. There are so many ITIs in the country offering this trade.
Duration:2 years
Stream:Engineering


Eligibility criteria

  • Students should be passed in Matriculation with Science (Physics & Chemistry) & Mathematics or its equivalent passed 10th class under 10+2 system with Science and Mathematics as subjects.

Benefits

  • After passing trade students can also go for further higher studies like Diploma/Degree courses for enhancing their overall qualification.
  • They can have jobs in computer software companies in form of maintaining electronic system and the like other.
  • They can work in computer/mobile repairing shops and also can apply for government jobs.

Advance Courses in Information Technology & Electronics System Maintenance Trade

  • Advance Diploma in Electronics
  • Advanced Diploma in Electronics and Electrical Equipments
  • Advanced Diploma in Information Technology
