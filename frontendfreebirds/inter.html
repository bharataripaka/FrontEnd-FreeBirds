Commerce stream in Intermediate

Commerce is one of the popular streams that a student choose after completing their Class 10th board exam. Commerce streams offer students a wide range of career options after completing Class 12th, that will give them a sense of financial security and make them successful. However, most of the students don’t acknowledge Commerce subject like science, a student needs to have a clear direction towards Commerce subject and become successful. Few students can have a positive and suited career in Commerce stream but might find it difficult to perform and achieve well in Commerce.

Commerce directly or indirectly is linked with business, especially with the distribution aspect. Commerce helps in creating a proper distribution channel and creates a market for surplus commodities. It has been already proven that good commerce performs an enormous role in the economic development of a country.

CEC and MEC group students have the option of studying various Arts fields. They can do their Bachelors in History, Economy, Political Science and many other sectors. The Career sector can also be diverse from teachers to Accountants and other jobs. When combined with an MBA they can reach highest positions in corporate sector. Also the government jobs can be achieved by them in their core sector with various exams. The particular career path can be suggested on detail but the career opportunities in general are good and diverse for the CEC and MEC Groups.

Eligibility criteria

  • Candidates can pursue a course in commerce at both Class 11 and Class 12 levels:
  • Intermediate : Candidates should clear SSC (10th class ) with minimum 50% - 60% marks from a recognized board.
  • course duration : 2 years
