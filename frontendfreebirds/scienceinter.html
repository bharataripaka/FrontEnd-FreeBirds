Science stream in Intermediate

Science is one of the popular streams that a student choose after completing their Class 10th board exam.Science streams offer students a wide range of career options after completing Class 12th, that will give them a sense of financial security and make them successful.

Science stream offers many lucrative career options such as engineering, medical, IT and you can even opt for research roles.The best advantage of taking science stream is, it keeps your options open. You might switch from science to commerce or science to arts. But it is not possible to do the other way around.aking science stream equips you with excellent problem-solving abilities.

Eligibility criteria

  • Candidates can pursue a course in Science at both Class 11 and Class 12 levels:
  • Intermediate : Candidates should clear SSC (10th class ) with minimum 50% - 60% marks from a recognized board.
  • course duration : 2 years

    • Courses offered in Science stream in intermediate

    MPC

    If students study MPC as the main subjects in the 12th standard, it is obvious for them to choose professional courses in Science.Students can pursue various graduate, postgraduate, diploma, and certificate courses in science subjects which lands them a successful career in science industries.

    • MPC stands for Mathematics,Physics and Chemistry
    • course duration : 2 years
    • Students can choose to study MPC group subjects after their Matriculation
    • MPC course offers various career opportunities in the Science stream
    • Students can pursue their diploma courses and certification courses in MPC subjects. After the successful completion of courses, employment opportunities increases
    • There are various top universities in India that offer various courses in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry subjects.
    BiPC

    If students study BiPC as the main subjects in the 12th standard, it is obvious for them to choose professional courses in Medicine. Students can pursue various graduate, postgraduate, diploma, and certificate courses in medicine which lands them a successful career in Medical Fields.

    • BiPC stands for Biology,Physics and Chemistry
    • Course duration:2 years
    • Students can choose to study BiPC group subjects after their Matriculation
    • BiPC course offers various career opportunities in the Medical stream
    • Students can pursue MBBS,Dentistry, Ayurveda ,Homeopathy etc which increases their employment opportunities in the medical field.
