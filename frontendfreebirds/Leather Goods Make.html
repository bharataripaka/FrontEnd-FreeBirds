Leather Goods Make


Leather Goods Maker or Footwear Management is a leather goods making vocational trade. The duration of trade is one year with two semesters of six months each. During the trade, students are introduced to tactics like making and repairing leather articles such as handbags, wallets, gloves, belts, harnesses and saddlery; select suitable types and grades of leather; draw patterns; cut leather to size and shape using special cutting machines, etc. Trade opens many careers in leather industry in different forms. Students can also go for higher degree courses.
Duration:1 years
Stream:Non-Engineering


Eligibility criteria

  • Students should be passed in 8th class from a recognised school board in the country.

Benefits

  • After passing trade, they are good at doing activities like fabricate products by cutting, sewing, gluing and bonding different sections of canvas and leather to make tents, awnings, rugs, etc.
  • They have good scopes for further higher studies as here they get good base for it and it will enhance their overall qualification.
  • They can work in shoes making factories/industries and other places manufacturing leather products.

Advance Courses in Leather Goods Maker Trade

  • Diploma in Leather Goods & Accessories Design
  • Diploma in Leather Goods Manufacturing
  • Diploma in Leather Technology
